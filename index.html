A bunch of browser-based apps that don't require a server. These can be run locally. Some don't even require an internet connection.

Complete

  • Sudoku helper mini-app
  • A canvas-based animated rendering of a fractal fern.
  • Simple AB-test visualiser
  • A comparative Box-Plot visualiser
  • Simple GeoLocation reader mini-app
  • DIY Browser-based Teleprompt
  • A production-line simulator
  • Some graph-formatting tools

In progress

  • Some SVG-based animations.
  • A crude bus simulator mini-app that needs controls and instructions added.
  • Some data-conversion tools
  • Attempt at logic-mapping software
